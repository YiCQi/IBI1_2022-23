Question: How have new cases and total cases developed over time in the UK?

Result: covid.py, line 61.

Answer:
Until approximately 2020-03-03, there were almost no new cases in the UK.
After 2020-03-03, the total number of cases increased and the rate of growth gradually increased.
The number of new cases per day also followed a roughly upward trend, peaking around 2020-03-28.
